About Blue Archive

Blue Archive is a gacha-based RPG developed by NAT Games and published by Yostar. Set in the fictional city of Kivotos, players take on the role of "Sensei," a counselor tasked with assisting students from various academies in resolving conflicts and challenges. The game is known for its stunning anime-style visuals, unique characters, and an engaging storyline that spans various genres, from comedy to drama.
